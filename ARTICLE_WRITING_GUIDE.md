# RenewBerry Article Writing Guide

Welcome to the RenewBerry Article Editor! This guide details the complete workflow for creating, editing, and publishing content on the platform.

## 1. Getting Started

To begin writing a new article:
1. Navigate to the **Creator Studio** or **Admin Dashboard**.
2. Click the **"Write New Article"** button (or the Pen icon in the sidebar).
3. You will be taken to the editor interface.

## 2. The Editor Interface

The RenewBerry editor is designed to be intuitive yet powerful.

### Main Areas
- **Title & Excerpt**: The first section allows you to set the headline and a short summary for search engines and cards.
- **Canvas**: The central writing area, designed to simulate a real page layout with a ruler for margins.
- **Toolbar**: Located at the top (sticky), containing all formatting and insertion tools.
- **Sidebar**: Contains publishing settings, tags, featured image, and SEO preview.
- **Stats Bar**: At the bottom of the editor, showing word count, reading time, and character limits.

### Distraction-Free Mode
Click the **Maximize** icon (top right of the canvas) to hide the sidebar and focus solely on your writing.

## 3. Writing & Formatting Tools

### Basic Formatting
Use the toolbar to apply standard formatting:
- **Bold** (Ctrl+B), **Italic** (Ctrl+I), **Underline** (Ctrl+U).
- **Headings**: H2 and H3 for section titles.
- **Lists**: Bulleted and Numbered lists.
- **Quote**: Blockquotes for emphasis.
- **Divider**: Horizontal rules to separate sections.

### Advanced Content Blocks
Insert special elements to make your article engaging:
- **Callouts**: Blue boxes for tips or key takeaways.
- **Fact Boxes**: Yellow boxes for "Did You Know?" trivia.
- **Code Blocks**: Syntax-highlighted blocks for technical code.
- **Tables**: Responsive tables with headers.

### Multimedia
- **Images**: Click the Image icon to upload or paste a URL. Set alt text for accessibility.
- **Videos**: Embed videos directly from the RenewBerry platform library using the Video icon.

### Structural Tools
- **Page Breaks**: Insert a visual page break to split long articles (simulated pagination).
- **Watermarks**: Add "Draft" or "Confidential" watermarks (Text or Image) with customizable opacity and rotation.

## 4. Writing Assistance

### Templates
Stuck on how to start? Click the **Templates** icon (file text) to choose from:
- **How-to Guide**: Step-by-step structure.
- **Listicle**: Numbered list format.
- **Opinion Piece**: Argumentative structure.

### AI Suggestions
Click the **Sparkles** icon next to the title to generate:
- Engaging Titles.
- Compelling Excerpts based on your topic.

### Grammar & Style
Click the **Checkmark** icon to open the Writing Assistant:
- **Readability Report**: Checks for long sentences, adverb usage, and passive voice.
- **Spell Check**: Toggle the native browser spell checker with the **ABC** button.

### Find & Replace
Use the **Search** icon in the toolbar to find and replace text within your article.

## 5. Workflow & Management

### Word Count Goals
Click on the word count in the bottom stats bar to set a target word count. A progress bar will track your progress.

### Version History
Made a mistake? Click the **History** icon to view auto-saved versions of your article. You can restore any previous version instantly.

### Table of Contents
Click the **List** icon to view an auto-generated Table of Contents based on your H2 and H3 headings. Click any item to jump to that section.

## 6. Publishing Settings (Sidebar)

### Status & Visibility
- **Status**: Switch between **Draft**, **Review**, and **Published**.
- **Visibility**: Choose **Public** (visible to everyone) or **Unlisted** (accessible via link only).
- **Schedule**: Set a future date for publication.

### Organization & SEO
- **Category & Tags**: Organize your content for discoverability.
- **Featured Image**: Upload a cover image (1200x600px recommended).
- **SEO Preview**: See how your article appears on Google, Twitter, and Facebook.

## 7. Saving & Publishing

- **Auto-Save**: The editor automatically saves your work locally every few seconds.
- **Preview**: Toggle **Preview Mode** to see exactly how your article will look on Desktop, Tablet, or Mobile devices.
- **Publish**: When ready, click the **Publish** (or "Save Changes") button to go live!

Happy Writing!
